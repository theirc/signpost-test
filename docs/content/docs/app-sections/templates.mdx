# Templates Section

## Overview

The Templates section is your comprehensive library of pre-built agent configurations designed to accelerate agent development. This guide covers everything from browsing and using existing templates to creating and sharing your own custom templates.

## 1. Templates Dashboard

![Templates Main Interface](/images/screenshots/templates/templates-main.png)
*Templates Main Interface - Browse and manage agent templates*

The templates dashboard provides:
- **Template Library**: Comprehensive collection of pre-built agents
- **Category Organization**: Templates organized by use case and industry
- **Search and Filter**: Find templates that match your needs
- **Template Preview**: See how templates work before using them
- **Custom Templates**: Your own saved template configurations
- **Community Templates**: Templates shared by other users

## 2. Template Categories

### Communication Templates
**Customer Service Agents:**
- **Support Assistant**: Handle customer inquiries and issues
- **FAQ Bot**: Answer frequently asked questions
- **Escalation Handler**: Route complex issues to human agents
- **Multilingual Support**: Provide support in multiple languages

**Sales and Marketing:**
- **Lead Qualifier**: Assess and qualify potential customers
- **Product Recommender**: Suggest products based on preferences
- **Email Responder**: Automated email responses
- **Social Media Manager**: Handle social media interactions

### Content Generation Templates
**Writing Assistants:**
- **Blog Writer**: Create blog posts and articles
- **Email Composer**: Draft professional emails
- **Report Generator**: Create business reports and summaries
- **Social Media Content**: Generate posts and captions

**Creative Content:**
- **Story Generator**: Create narratives and stories
- **Marketing Copy**: Develop advertising and marketing content
- **Technical Writer**: Create documentation and manuals
- **Translator**: Translate content between languages

### Data Processing Templates
**Analysis and Research:**
- **Data Analyzer**: Process and analyze datasets
- **Research Assistant**: Gather and summarize information
- **Document Processor**: Extract insights from documents
- **Trend Analyzer**: Identify patterns and trends

**Information Management:**
- **Knowledge Curator**: Organize and categorize information
- **Content Summarizer**: Create summaries of long content
- **Question Answerer**: Provide answers from knowledge bases
- **Fact Checker**: Verify information accuracy

### Workflow Automation Templates
**Business Process Automation:**
- **Task Orchestrator**: Coordinate multiple business tasks
- **Approval Workflow**: Handle approval processes
- **Notification Manager**: Send and manage notifications
- **Data Integrator**: Connect and sync different systems

**Administrative Assistants:**
- **Calendar Manager**: Schedule and manage appointments
- **Email Organizer**: Sort and prioritize emails
- **Document Manager**: Organize and track documents
- **Meeting Assistant**: Manage meetings and notes

## Getting Started

1. **Browse Templates**: Explore the template library
2. **Select Template**: Choose a template that fits your needs
3. **Preview Template**: Review the template configuration
4. **Create Agent**: Use the template to create a new agent
5. **Customize**: Modify the agent to fit your requirements

## Best Practices

- Start with the closest matching template
- Review template capabilities before use
- Customize templates to fit your specific needs
- Save successful customizations as new templates
- Document template modifications for team reference
